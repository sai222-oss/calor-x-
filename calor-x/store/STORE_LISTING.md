## Full Description (≤ 4000 characters)

### English

**Calor X — AI Nutrition Tracker for Arabic Cuisine**

Take a photo of any meal and get instant, accurate nutritional data — optimized for the foods you actually eat.

**📸 Scan Any Dish**
Just snap a photo. Calor X uses GPT-4o Vision to identify your dish and return a full breakdown of calories, protein, fat, and carbohydrates in seconds.

**🥙 Built for Arab & Middle Eastern Food**
Generic calorie apps don't understand Kabsa, Mandi, Mansaf, Shawarma, or Tagine. Calor X does. Our AI is tuned with region-specific heuristics for shared platters, bread portions, olive-oil-rich dishes, and more.

**⚡ Instant Results, Even Offline**
Results for previously scanned dishes load instantly from our smart similarity cache. No internet needed for repeat meals.

**📊 Track Your Progress**
Log daily meals, monitor calorie and macro trends, and get visual progress charts over time.

**🤖 AI Dietary Coach**
Chat with your personal nutrition coach to get meal suggestions, portion guidance, and answers to diet questions tailored to your cuisine preferences.

**🌍 Multi-Region Support**
Tailored for users in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Morocco, and the broader MENA region.

**🔒 Privacy First**
Your data is stored securely. Images are processed once and cached — we never sell your data.
